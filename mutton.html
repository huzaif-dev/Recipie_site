Mutton Curry

This hearty mutton curry is full of robust spices and slow-cooked to perfection for tender, flavorful meat.

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton pieces (bone-in preferred)
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste, then tomatoes, and cook until soft.
  3. Add yogurt and all spices. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add mutton and mix well. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Add water, cover, and simmer for 1-1.5 hours or until tender.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
