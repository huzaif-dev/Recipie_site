Mexican Tinga

Chicken Tinga is a classic Mexican dish made with shredded chicken simmered in a rich, smoky tomato-chipotle sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Tostadas or tortillas for serving

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté the onion until soft and translucent.
  2. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Blend tomatoes and chipotle peppers until smooth. Add to the pan.
  4. Season with oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add chicken and chicken broth. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  6. Serve on warm tortillas or crispy tostadas with toppings like avocado, cheese, or sour cream.
