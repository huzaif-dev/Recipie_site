Chicken Tikka

Tender, smoky, and bursting with flavor, this chicken tikka is perfect as a snack or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cubed)
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for at least 2 hours.
  2. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  3. Grill or bake at 200°C (400°F) for 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney or lemon wedges.
