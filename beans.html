Brown Stew Garbanzo Beans

A comforting Caribbean-style stew made with hearty garbanzo beans simmered in a rich, savory brown sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked garbanzo beans (chickpeas)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water or vegetable broth as needed

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot, sauté onions, garlic, and bell pepper until soft.
  2. Add tomato, tomato paste, thyme, and allspice. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cooked beans and soy sauce, stir well to combine.
  4. Add water or broth to cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Adjust seasoning and serve warm with rice or bread.
