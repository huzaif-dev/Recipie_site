Authentic Biryani Recipe

This rich and aromatic Biryani is made with layers of fragrant basmati rice, spiced meat, and caramelized onions. Perfect for celebrations!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or mutton (marinated)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Spices: cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaf
  • Saffron in warm milk

Instructions

  1. Soak basmati rice and cook until 70% done.
  2. Fry onions until golden and set aside.
  3. Cook marinated meat with spices until tender.
  4. Layer meat and rice in a pot with fried onions and saffron milk.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-30 mins.
