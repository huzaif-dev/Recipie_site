Spicy Chicken

A flavorful and comforting chicken curry perfect with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala)
  • Fresh coriander

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions until golden, add ginger garlic paste.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add chicken and spices, cook until done.
  4. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
